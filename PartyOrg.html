Party Summary

The main adventuring party is made up of 3 characters, a Wizard, Ranger, and Cleric. The details of these characters are listed below

Cleric

No ordinary priest or temple servant, a cleric is imbued with the power to wield divine magic to defend and heal companions.

Full Class Details

  • Specalizes in healing and divine spells
  • Uses blunt weapons and spells to deal damage
  • Uses heavy armor to protect themselves
  • Must rely on magic to be effective

Ranger

Rangers are skilled hunters, guides, and trackers. They are members of a secretive order that protects the wilderness from incursions.

Full Class Details

  • Specalizes in ranged damage and minor nature spells
  • Uses ranged weapons to deal superior ranged damage
  • Uses high dexterity and light armor to avoid damage
  • Provide little utility if not outdoors

Wizard

Wizards are the masters of arcane magic. They cast spells of rolling fire, arcing lightning, or creeping shadow, and more.

Full Class Details

  • Specalizes in utility and area damage with Spells
  • Uses Spells to deal elemental damage
  • Uses Spells to protect themselves and the party
  • Easily falls in martial combat
